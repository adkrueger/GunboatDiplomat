Create Playlist: 
	Enter a playlist name in the text field under the "Playlists" section, then click the New Playlist button.

Show Playlists: 
	Playlists are displayed upon page load.

Delete Playlist: 
	Click the Delete button below the Playlist you wish to delete.

Upload Video Segment: 
	Select a properly formatted .ogg file using the browse button 
	in "Video Segments". Next, enter a character and quote for the video segment.
	Next, click the upload button.
	
Delete Video Segment: 
	Click the Delete button below the desired video segment.

Append Local Video Segment: 
	Click the Append button next to the desired video segment.
	Then, enter the name of the playlist you would like to add it to.

Append Remote Video Segment: 
	To append a remote video segment, one must first search for the 
	remote video segment. Once it appears, click the "Append to Playlist" button and enter the 
	playlist name you wish to append the remote video segment to.

Remove Video Segment From Playlist: 
	Click Remove VS button under the desired playlist, then enter the index of the video segment 
	you would like to remove (starting at 0).

Show Local Video Segments: 
	Local video segments are displayed upon page load.

Search Video Segments: 
	Searching video segments is an "and/or" type of search. If you enter 
	just a character then it will only search for a character, which is the same for a quote. 
	If you enter both, the search will search for both substrings. Search searches in both
	remote and local video segments.
	
Play Playlist: 
	Click the play button below the desired playlist.

Mark Video Segment for Public Use: 
	Click the Mark button below the desired local video segment.
	The video will subsequently be displayed as marked in the "Remotes" section of the webpage.
	
Unmark Video Segment for Public Use: 
	Click the Unmark button below the desired local video segment.
	The video will subsequently be displayed as unmarked in the "Remotes" section of the webpage.

Register Remote URL: 
	Enter a URL containing an API key in the Remotes section of the admin webpage.
	Next, click add URL.
	
Unregister Remote URL: 
	If Remote URLs are registered, one can click the Unregister button to 
	unregister the desired remote URL.
